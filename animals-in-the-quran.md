Animals Chapter or Surah name.
- Chapter 2: Bakara, or the Heifer
- Chapter 6: Anam, or Cattle
- Chapter 16: Nahl, or the Bee
- Chapter 27: Naml, or the Ants
- Chapter 29: Ankabut, or the Spider
